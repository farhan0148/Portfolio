NYP Floorball Website

A specialized recruitment platform designed to showcase the Nanyang Polytechnic Floorball CCA and drive student enrollment.

Interface & Experience

Role

Web Developer

Tech

HTML, CSS, JS

Focus

UI Efficiency

Timeline

Semester Project

The Solution

Commissioned as a Nanyang Polytechnic assignment, the goal was to design a compelling digital gateway for the NYP Floorball CCA. Tasked with boosting student recruitment, I created a vibrant community hub that showcases the team's competitive spirit, training culture, and achievements. The website serves as the primary touchpoint for prospective members to explore player stats and trial information through a high-performance, mobile-first interface.

Key Contributions

Strategic UX

Designed an engaging interface tailored to NYP students, balancing high-energy visuals with clear calls-to-action for CCA sign-ups.

CCA Identity System

Developed an organized roster system to highlight current players, fostering a sense of team identity for prospective members.

Student-Centric Mobile Design

Prioritized a seamless mobile experience, acknowledging that the target student demographic accesses CCA info primarily on the go.

Engagement Loops

Integrated news feeds and social components to showcase a thriving community, making the CCA more approachable to newcomers.

Reflection & Learning

  • Building a recruitment-focused site taught me how to align design choices with organizational goals (increasing CCA membership).
  • Using Bootstrap allowed me to create a professional-grade landing page that meets NYP academic standards.
  • I learned to translate the "energy" of a physical sport into a digital experience through strategic layout and imagery.
  • User testing revealed how students navigate for info, helping me refine the Information Architecture for trial schedules.
  • This project strengthened my ability to act as both a developer and a digital storyteller for the NYP community.
