Project Vision

MediaFly was designed as a response to social isolation. The project involved deep user research and rapid prototyping, culminating in a public presentation at the Apple Store Marina Bay Sands.

Key Contributions

Swift Prototyping

Developed a functional prototype using Swift Playgrounds, implementing core messaging features and real-time interaction flows.

UI/UX Design

Created high-fidelity mock-ups and user flow diagrams, conducting research to ensure features specifically addressed peer connection needs.

Public Speaking

Effectively communicated the project vision and social impact during a concept demo at the Apple Store to instructors and the public.

Team Leadership

Led a team of 4 developers, ensuring alignment between design goals and technical prototype limitations.
